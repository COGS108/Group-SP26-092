Shows the composition of the athlete data after filling missing team-event sports from the roster-page supplement. A large sport can heavily influence the overall birth-month pattern.

Explores whether medal-winning athlete appearances cluster by birth quarter. This is descriptive only because medal outcomes are sparse.

Scatter-matrix view of numeric variables. This is a broad relationship scan for age, birth timing, event count, and medal count.
